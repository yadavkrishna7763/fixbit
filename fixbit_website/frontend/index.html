FAQ

Common questions

Is FixBit free to use? +

Yes, completely free for both users and shops. We charge zero commission and take no cut from transactions. FixBit earns through optional premium shop listings in the future.

How are shops verified? +

Every shop goes through a manual registration process including business proof verification. Shops with repeated negative reviews are removed from the platform.

How fast do shops reply? +

Shops receive instant notifications when a new message arrives. Many conversations begin within minutes during business hours.

Do I pay on FixBit or at the shop? +

You pay directly at the repair shop — no money goes through FixBit. This keeps the process transparent and means zero hidden platform fees on your transaction.

Do I need to book through FixBit? +

No. FixBit focuses on discovery and direct messaging. You coordinate the final repair details directly with the shop.
